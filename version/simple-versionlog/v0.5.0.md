# ReviveL 0.5.0 — What’s new (simple)

**Released:** 2026-07-15

This is a big step forward. You can browse channels, upload to LBRY more easily, and open more than just videos.

### In plain language

- **Channels tab** — Browse popular channels, or see channels that belong to your own wallet.
- **Upload tab** — Publish files from the extension, create a new channel, or pick one of your existing channels.
- **Right-click Quick Upload** — Right-click an image anywhere on the web and upload it to LBRY in one step (or open a form if you turn automatic mode off in Settings).
- **Images, audio, and files** — The player is no longer video-only. Pictures and audio play properly; other files can be opened or downloaded.
- **Library improvements** — Split into Recently watched, My uploads, and Saved items.
- **Smarter thumbnails** — Missing thumbnails get a colored placeholder; channel items show a clear “Channel” badge.
- **Publishing that works better with Companion** — File staging, channel creation, and cleaner publish settings. Quick uploads stake a small but valid amount (**0.0001 LBC**) so they are not rejected as “dust.”
- **Wallet history polish** — Transaction list and status details are clearer.
- **UI polish** — Channel list rows stay compact: title on the left, “Channel” badge on the right.

### Who this is for

Anyone who wants LBRY in the browser: casual browsing still works with no daemon; full upload and wallet features need the ReviveL Companion / local lbrynet.
