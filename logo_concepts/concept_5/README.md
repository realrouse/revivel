# Concept 5 Revisions - Open Book Glowing (More Abstract & Minimal)

**Original chosen:** logo_concepts/concept_05.jpg  
Description: Simple lines resembling an open book which is glowing.

These 10 new revisions are pushed further toward **higher abstraction and minimalism**:
- Extremely reduced to basic geometric lines and forms
- Fewer elements, bolder strokes, less detail
- Focused on essential "open book + upward glow" silhouette
- Designed to be iconic at very small sizes (16x16+)
- Flat, high-contrast teal/cyan on dark navy
- No shading, minimal fills, pure vector logo mark style
